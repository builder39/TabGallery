TabGallery

Privacy Policy

Overview

TabGallery is a browser extension that turns your new tab page into a personal photo slideshow. We are committed to your privacy. TabGallery does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data or user information of any kind.

Data Collection

TabGallery does not collect any of the following:

  • Personally identifiable information
  • Photos or image content
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Location data
  • Authentication or financial information
  • Usage statistics or analytics

Local Storage

Photos you upload are stored exclusively in your browser's local IndexedDB storage on your own device. Your settings (slideshow interval, shuffle, display preferences) are stored in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never accessible to us or any third party.

Permissions

TabGallery requests no browser permissions beyond overriding the new tab page. It makes no network requests and has no access to your browsing activity, tabs, or any other browser data.

Third Parties

TabGallery does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external APIs. No data is ever sent to any server.

Children's Privacy

TabGallery does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated policy will be published at this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach out via the extension's listing page on the Chrome Web Store or Firefox Add-ons.
